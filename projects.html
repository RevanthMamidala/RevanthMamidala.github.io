Climate Change Mitigation and Bioenergy Production Analysis

Duration: 2020-2022 Status: Completed Scope: Global Analysis

Research Objective

Analyze the economic and environmental trade-offs of large-scale bioenergy production under different climate change scenarios across four major agricultural economies.

Key Methodologies

  • Global Change Assessment Model (GCAM) integration
  • Multi-scenario climate impact analysis (RCP 2.6, 4.5, 6.0)
  • Land use change economic modeling
  • Biomass production potential assessment

Key Findings

  • Brazil shows highest bioenergy potential under all scenarios
  • India faces significant land-food competition constraints
  • USA demonstrates most stable production under climate stress
  • China requires policy intervention for sustainable scaling
